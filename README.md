RothMethods is the only Applied Ontological Method that makes the Blindspot visible and transforms it permanently — so accomplished people stop performing from their machinery and start performing from who they truly are.

- **Applied** — it works. It produces measurable, observable, permanent change in how people perform.

- **Method** — it is precise, repeatable, and teachable. It has architecture. It has mechanics. It delivers.
