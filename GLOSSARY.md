# Glossary of Proprietary Terms

All terms defined here are proprietary to RothMethods. Definitions are precise and canonical — not approximate.

---

## Applied Ontological Method

A Technology that operates at the level of being — who a person is — not at the level of knowing or doing. Applied means it works: it produces measurable, observable, permanent change. Ontological means it reaches the source. Method means it is precise, repeatable, and teachable.

---

## The Blindspot

The machinery of Human Nature operating exactly as it came together accidentally from birth. Five components — Aversion, Avoidance, Adaptation, Attachment, Addiction — each with precise mechanics, each feeding the next in a self-manufacturing loop. Until it is seen, it is more powerful, progressive, and fatal.

---

## Human Nature

The machinery built by accident from birth, optimized for survival. Its five components are Aversion, Avoidance, Adaptation, Attachment, and Addiction. It is not a flaw. It is survival intelligence operating at the wrong speed in the wrong direction.

---

## Human Spirit

The Core Values already present within every human being: Aspiration, Commitment, Conversation, Contribution, Passion. Not aspirational — architectural. They exist. The Blindspot obscures them.

---

## Human Being

Human Nature + Human Spirit = Human Being. The full equation. Neither side alone is the whole person.

---

## Aversion

The first component of the Blindspot. Three mechanics: Critical Evaluation / Uncompromised Judgement / Total Condemnation.

---

## Avoidance

The second component of the Blindspot. Three mechanics: Distorted Perception / Inappropriate Reaction / Unforeseen Consequence. Avoidance is intelligence operating at survival speed in a counterproductive manner to keep you safe, by keeping you small.

---

## Adaptation

The third component of the Blindspot. Three mechanics: Unwitting Perpetration / Immediate Justification / Total Negation.

---

## Attachment

The fourth component of the Blindspot. Three mechanics: Contradiction / Resignation / Fusion. Until you see your Attachment clearly, you will continue to believe that the boundaries of your machinery are the boundaries of your life.

---

## Addiction

The fifth component of the Blindspot. Three mechanics: Denial / Deception / Delusion. Addiction is the architectural glue — the binding agent that makes the entire Blindspot structure self-perpetuating. This is how you tie the knot on the blindfold.

---

## Aspiration

The first Core Value. Three dynamics: Declaring Vision / Defining Mission / Creating Impact. Aspiration is the opposite of Ambition. Ambition is machinery-driven. Aspiration is Spirit-driven.

---

## Commitment

The second Core Value. Three dynamics: Intention / Attention / Inspection. Commitment is how the Human Spirit guides action. Obligation is what the machinery uses to control behavior. They are not the same.

---

## Conversation

The third Core Value. Three dynamics: Creativity / Compassion / Collaboration. Conversation doesn't end with agreement. It ends with alignment.

---

## Contribution

The fourth Core Value. Three dynamics: Fostering Development / Enhancing Performance / Enabling Fulfillment.

---

## Passion

The fifth Core Value. Three dynamics: What have I accomplished / What do I need to do / What's possible if I do that. Passion is the path.

---

## Maximizing Performance

The natural result of a person performing from who they truly are rather than from their machinery. Not a technique applied from the outside. The more a person uses the Tool to practice the Method, the more they move toward Maximizing Performance.

---

## Pre-Print Training

Training architecture that is immersive, not informative — eductive, not directive. It allows the recipient to receive, retain, and replicate the learning more precisely and more permanently. The opposite of literary training.

---

## Literary Training

The incumbent model of learning — text-based, informative, directive. It delivers content to the mind. It does not reach the place where the Blindspot lives.

---

## The Intellectual Map Trap

Understanding without behavior change. The machinery adapts to conceptual awareness and uses the intellectual map to become more sophisticated. The map hasn't changed the behavior because the map is literary. Transformation is pre-print.

---

## The Howard Paradox

1000% in fulfillment produces 1000% in performance. Named for Howard — a commercial real estate broker who was told to pursue sailing 1000% and made $2.4M that year. Fulfillment is not the reward for achievement. It is the engine of it.

---

## Advisory Selling Method

The Applied Ontological Method extended into the domain of selling. Selling redefined as Inceptive, not Deceptive. Eductive, not Aggressive. Every human conversation is a selling conversation.

---

## Eductive

Drawing out — not pushing in. The opposite of directive. The mode of influence that works with a person's own internal commitment rather than against their resistance.
